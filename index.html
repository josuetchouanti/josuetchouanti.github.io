I am a postdoctoral researcher in Unité de Mathématiques Pures et Appliquées (UMPA), CNRS UMR 5669 - ENS Lyon under the supervision of Hélène Leman, Hélène Morlon and Amaury Lambert, currently working on multiscale approaches in population genetics.


The core of my research activity is the deterministic/stochastic modelling and study of complex phenomena from biology and life sciences. More precisely, I am interested in:
  • population genetics;
  • the metabolic heterogeneity in small and large cell populations;
  • developing statistical tests of independence on point processes for the detection of neural synchronization;
  • population dynamics with disasters and migration within a network;
  • the co-evolution and control of phage-bacteria interactions for applications in phagotherapy;
  • age-structured epidemiological models and their control.
