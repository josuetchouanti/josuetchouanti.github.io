About me

I am an associate professor (Maître de conférences) at Institut de Mathématiques de Toulouse in the Probability team. The core of my research activity is the construction and study of deterministic/stochastic models of complex phenomena from Biology and life sciences. More precisely, my work focuses on mathematical tools for applications in ecology, adaptive dynamics, epidemiology and cell metabolism.
